\# Russell Brunson — LinkedIn Posts



\## Post 1

Success doesn't come from one big move. It comes from doing the small things, 

a thousand times, without quitting. Consistency beats intensity. Every. Single. Time.

Whether you're building a funnel, a brand, or a habit, don't underestimate the 

power of small actions compounded over time.



\## Post 2

Every small business owner is Cape Verde. You're walking onto a field against 

competitors with bigger budgets, bigger teams, and longer track records.

Size doesn't decide the outcome. System does. Preparation does.

You will almost never beat the giants by trying to be a bigger version of them.

You beat them by being tighter, faster, and more disciplined.



\## Post 3

Every hour you spend working in the business is an hour stolen from working 

on the business. They answer the emails. Fix the fires. But in the process 

they rob themselves of the one thing that moves the needle: Vision.

The new funnel. The new offer. The strategic shift that could double revenue in 90 days.
