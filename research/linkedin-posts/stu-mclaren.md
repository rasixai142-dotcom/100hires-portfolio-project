\## Post 1

The math on this one is brutal and kind at the same time.

Wait, and a year from now you're wishing you'd started.

\## Post 2

Picture launching from scratch every single month. That's the model most 

creators are stuck in, and it's exhausting. Membership flips it. 

Last month's work shows up to help this month. You build on momentum 

instead of replacing it.



\## Post 3

Everyone obsesses over getting new people in the door. The quieter, 

bigger win is the people already inside. Keep them happy and they stay 

for years. That's the difference between a launch you have to repeat 

and a business that compounds.
