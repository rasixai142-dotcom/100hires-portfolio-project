\# Jeff Walker — LinkedIn Posts



\## Post 1

If anyone can use a free AI tool to get a personalized answer to any question 

in seconds… are courses dead? We have to assume the value of information is 

trending towards zero. What your customers are REALLY buying in an AI world 

is transformation, not information. People using AI to solve their problems 

for free could be your best customers.
