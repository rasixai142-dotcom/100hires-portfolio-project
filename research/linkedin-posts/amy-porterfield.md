\# Amy Porterfield — LinkedIn Posts



\## Post 1

Your business is always teaching you something about yourself.

Who you are when a client leaves. Who you are when you charge what you're worth.

Who you are when you finally let go of doing it all alone.

That's the part nobody talks about — the growth that happens inside the business,

not just because of it.



\## Post 2

What looked like strategic thinking was actually fear in a different outfit.

Sitting on the launch. Tweaking the offer one more time. Waiting until it's perfect.

That's not strategy. That's protection. And it's costing you.



\## Post 3

You have to stop spending like a hobbyist.

If you're running a real business, investing like one matters.

Hobbyists spend on what feels good. Business owners invest in what works.
